(**0.0196**) is **2.55× lower** than the frozen constant-v1 counterfactual (**0.0499**).

| pair | type | scaled err | constant err |
|---|---|---|---|
| T8 | null (ohc unchanged) | 0.00007 | 0.03973 |

nails the null/capped pairs (where the field does not move) while the state-blind constant predicts

operator-consented target-sensitive switch genuinely improved the model, *specifically at predicting
when the world will not change.* (`PILOT-ARC-2-ANALYSIS.md`.) **Honest ceiling: n = 4.**
