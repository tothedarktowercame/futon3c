## Change

`futon3c.aif.flight-record` now emits structured `:ground` maps for every ground
the producer derives. The required discriminator is `:ground/kind`; each kind
carries the witness fields already present at close time.

Examples:

- `{:ground/kind :begin-scan ...}`
- `{:ground/kind :organ-ref :organ :warrant}`
- `{:ground/kind :ref :ref "..."}`
- `{:ground/kind :inline-evidence ...}`
- `{:ground/kind :model-output ...}`
- `{:ground/kind :evidence-ref ...}`
- `{:ground/kind :derived ...}`
- `{:ground/kind :backfilled-frame ...}`

Pre-wrapped pilot-supplied cells still pass through unchanged. That preserves
the existing contract: the composer must not pretend to understand a pilot's
already-authored ground. The producer-side gap is closed because new
composer-derived grounds are no longer prose.
