A buyer evaluating a custom software vendor is doing one of two things, roughly: either (a) they have a precisely-specified deliverable in mind and they're price-shopping against other vendors who could ship the same thing, or (b) they have a capability gap and they're trying to figure out which vendor most plausibly closes it.
